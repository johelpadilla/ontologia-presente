\section{Empirical Validation in Synthetic Systems}

\subsection{Coupled Logistic Map}

Four-component coupled logistic map ($r=3.8$, weak coupling, controlled injections of structural change). Generation of controlled Layer 1 + Layer 2 realizations under different coupling and noise regimes. Statistics of episode number, duration, Joint Score, and outcome separation.

\subsection{Lorenz Attractor (discretized, multi-variable)}

Analogous analysis on the canonical chaotic attractor with added variables or projections to emulate modular structure. Comparison of episode statistics and transition signatures.

\subsection{Regime Contrasts}

Explicit comparison of “San-Juan-like” (high-intensity, frequent sustained Layer 2) vs. “Iquitos-like” (rare, short Layer 2) synthetic ensembles.
